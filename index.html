The Arcade

Vibe-coded whims. Each game is a single HTML file — no frameworks, no build steps, just canvas and curiosity.

Dream Logic

roguelike leaderboard

Endless fog-of-war puzzle roguelike. Navigate procedural dreamscapes, collect memories, and climb the leaderboard.

Knot Equivalence

webgl math

3D knot identification game. Two knots shown side by side in random orientations — guess whether they're the same type.

Planning Joker

cards

Deckbuilder roguelike in corporate hell. Survive sprints by playing skill cards alongside opinionated AI teammates. Don't drown in tech debt.

Ram Butt

local multiplayer

A two-player game of stubbornness. Ram your opponent off the field in head-to-head combat.

Motion Storm

webcam

Webcam-powered physics game. Your body movements create force fields that push balls around.

Snowball

arcade

Outrun the avalanche. Roll downhill, lean and tuck for speed, hit ramps for boosts. Get massive or get buried.

Railgrind

arcade

Mine-cart climb through 9 Dante-style circles. One button: jump. Falling drops you to the rail below — only the spike pit kills.

Sprout & Splice

genetics sim

Grow seeds, harvest blooms, and splice two parents to cross their genes. Dominant traits show, recessives hide — discover new species and hunt for rare glowing variants.
