Planning Joker

Estimate. Strategize. Ship.

How to Play

You're a Scrum Master leading a small dev team through sprint planning. Each sprint has an Epic with 3 tickets. Your job: work with your teammates to shrink each ticket's bloated story points.

Your Team: You'll have 2-3 AI teammates on a video call. Each has their own personality, cards, and mood. You take turns playing cards in a circle.

The Catch: Two things decide your fate:

🟢 Team Mood — teammates' average mood. Too low = they disconnect!

🔵 Company Satisfaction — estimates too LOW = "are you sandbagging?"

If all teammates disconnect or satisfaction hits zero, game over.

Each Ticket (Turn-Based):

1. On your turn, click a Skill Card to play it (one card per turn)

2. Your teammates take their turns automatically

3. Combos trigger from cards played in the same round by anyone!

4. Hit Submit Estimate when the points look right, or Pass to skip

Epic Boss: After 3 tickets, face the Epic itself — a multi-phase boss battle with interruptions!

Sweet Spot: 1-5 points per item. That's the Goldilocks zone.

Survive 5 sprints to get promoted.
